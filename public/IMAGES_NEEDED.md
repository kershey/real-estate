# Images Needed for Homepage

To complete the homepage design, please add the following images to the `public/` folder:

## Required Images

1. **hero-house.jpg**
   - A modern luxury home exterior photo (dusk/evening lighting preferred)
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080px or larger
   - Used as: Hero section background

2. **property-card.jpg**
   - Modern home interior or exterior photo for the property card
   - Recommended size: 800x600px or larger
   - Used as: Property card overlay on hero section

3. **interior-dining.jpg**
   - Modern interior dining/living space with wooden beams
   - Recommended size: 1600x1000px or larger
   - Used as: Featured image in "Connecting People" section
